Generate key (One time only):
keytool -genkey -v -keystore android_app_keystores/good_or_bad_app.keystore -alias GoodOrBad -keyalg RSA -keysize 2048 -validity 10000

Sign app with key:
jarsigner -verbose -sigalg SHA1withRSA -digestalg SHA1 -keystore /MyWork/android_app_keystores/good_or_bad_app.keystore GoodorBad-0.1-release-unsigned.apk GoodOrBad

ZipAlign the apk:
zipalign -v 4 your_project_name-unaligned.apk your_project_name.apk
